# Where to Add DNS Records in KonsoleH

## üéØ Exact Location Steps

### Step 1: Log into KonsoleH
1. Go to **https://konsoleh.co.za** (or your KonsoleH login URL)
2. Log in with your KonsoleH account credentials

### Step 2: Find DNS Management
The exact location depends on your KonsoleH interface, but look for one of these:

**Option A: cPanel Interface**
1. Look for **"DNS Zone Editor"** or **"Zone Editor"** in the main menu
2. Or go to **"Domains"** ‚Üí **"DNS Zone Editor"**
3. Or **"Advanced"** ‚Üí **"DNS Zone Editor"**

**Option B: Direct DNS Management**
1. Look for **"DNS Management"** or **"DNS Records"**
2. Or **"Domains"** ‚Üí **"DNS Management"**
3. Or **"Advanced"** ‚Üí **"DNS Management"**

**Option C: Domain Management**
1. Click on **"Domains"** in the main menu
2. Find **"spana.co.za"** in the list
3. Click on it or look for **"Manage DNS"** or **"DNS Records"** button

### Step 3: Select Your Domain
1. Once in DNS Zone Editor/DNS Management
2. Select **"spana.co.za"** from the domain dropdown (if shown)
3. You should see a list of existing DNS records (A, MX, CNAME, TXT, etc.)

### Step 4: Add New Records
1. Look for **"Add Record"** or **"+"** button
2. Or find a section that says **"Add DNS Record"**
3. Click it to add each of the 4 Resend records
